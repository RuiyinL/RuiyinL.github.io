About Me
======

I am a Hongyi Postdoctoral Fellow in the School of Computer Science at **Wuhan University**. I received dual Ph.D. degrees in Software Engineering from the Faculty of Science and Engineering at the **University of Groningen** (<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Groningen" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">RUG</a>, Groningen, the Netherlands) and the School of Computer Science at **Wuhan University** (<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wuhan_University" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">WHU</a>, Wuhan, China).

My research focuses on software engineering, with particular emphasis on intelligent approaches to requirements engineering, software architecture, and empirical studies. I have published in leading venues such as ICSE, ICPC, ICSA, EASE, APSEC, TOSEM, IST, and JSEP. I am the PI of an NSFC Youth Fund (Type C) project and have participated in several national and provincial research programs. I was selected for the Special Project for Introducing Overseas Postdoctoral Talent (2025) and the Hubei Provincial Postdoctoral Talent Introduction Program (2024). I welcome collaboration with colleagues in academia and industry.


Research Interests
======
- AI for Software Engineering (AI4SE)
- Requirements Engineering
- Software Architecture Design and Evolution
- Mining Software Repositories
- Empirical Software Engineering

个人简介
======

现任武汉大学计算机学院弘毅博士后，获格罗宁根大学（<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Groningen" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">RUG</a>，荷兰格罗宁根）与武汉大学（<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wuhan_University" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">WHU</a>，中国武汉）软件工程双博士学位。研究聚焦软件工程，重点关注智能化方法在需求工程、软件体系结构与经验软件工程中的应用。在 ICSE、ICPC、ICSA、EASE、APSEC 等国际会议及 TOSEM、IST、JSEP 等期刊发表系列论文；主持国家自然科学基金青年基金（C 类）项目 1 项，参与多项国家级/省部级科研项目；入选 2025 年“海外博士后引才专项”，2024 年“湖北省博士后尖端人才引进项目”。欢迎与学界、业界同仁开展交流与合作。
